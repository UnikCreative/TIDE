Abing Chukwu-Iza (GS)

Abing Chukwu Iza (MTRCN) has a Bachelor's degree in Education with History as a teaching subject. He is currently studying for a master’s degree in Peace and Conflict Studies at the University of Calabar, Nigeria. As a professional teacher, he is involved in the training and retraining of teachers in Africa at Teachers Continuous Training Institute Biase, Nigeria. Abing is a committed social liberal and persuasive communicator keen on inspiring young people to achieving their set-goals in life. Equally, he is deeply sensitive to the economic needs of disadvantaged people throughout the world. And he has a marked interest in bridging the gaps between the government and the people in the polity.
